About

A joule is a unit of work or energy. This website is a collection of my work — paintings, drawings, and digital media.

Born in Naples, Italy, I spent my formative years on the East Coast of the United States. I received my BFA in Painting and Drawing from the University of the Arts in Philadelphia. Currently live in San Diego, California.

This site is built and hosted entirely on AWS as part of the Cloud Resume Challenge. It runs on a fully serverless, globally distributed architecture — highly available, cost-efficient (under $1/month), and built with security in mind. Security controls include HTTPS enforcement, strict browser security headers, private origin access, geo-restriction, threat detection, and full audit logging. Project details are available on GitHub.
